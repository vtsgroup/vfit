───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
IMPORTANT: DO's AND DON'Ts
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

DO ✅
  • Copy dashboard patterns verbatim (they're production-grade)
  • Reuse DSIcon for all icons (v4 support is built-in)
  • Study Framer Motion patterns in mobile-nav.tsx
  • Test on real mobile device with haptic
  • Use design tokens (never raw hex values)
  • Verify safe areas on notch devices (iPhone X+, Android with gesture nav)

DON'T ❌
  • Don't create custom icons (DSIcon handles it)
  • Don't hard-code colors (use Tailwind tokens)
  • Don't animate width/height (transform + opacity only)
  • Don't skip accessibility (aria-labels, focus states)
  • Don't forget haptic feedback
  • Don't ignore safe areas (mobile notches + gesture bars)
